Ingredients Of Red Sauce Pasta
For the Tomato Sauce:
5-6 tomatoes (diced)
1 garlic clove
1 onion
1/2 cup water
1 Bay leaf
1/2 tsp sugar
4-5 Basil leaves
1 tbsp onions, chopped
1/2 tbsp garlic, chopped
to taste salt
Oil (to saute)
For the Pasta:
110 gms pasta
3 cups water
A pinch of salt
